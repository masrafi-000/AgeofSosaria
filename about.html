About Age of Sosaria

A shard built from passion, experience, and respect for classic Ultima Online.

Age of Sosaria was created with a simple goal: to bring back the feeling of classic Ultima Online while offering thoughtful improvements and custom content designed for long-term enjoyment.

This shard is not a quick project or a short-lived experiment. It is the result of years of experience, careful design decisions, and a deep respect for what made Ultima Online special.

Our Philosophy

We believe the best shards are built around meaningful progression, player interaction, and a world that feels alive.

Every system is designed with balance, fairness, and longevity in mind. We avoid shortcuts, overpowered rewards, and features that undermine the integrity of the game.

Custom, Not Chaotic

Age of Sosaria features custom mechanics and content, but always within the spirit of classic gameplay. Our goal is to enhance the experience, not replace it.

You won't find excessive handouts, pay-to-win systems, or features that trivialize progression. Success is earned through time, effort, and smart choices.

Active Development

The world of Sosaria is always evolving. We regularly improve systems, add new content, and listen to community feedback to shape the future of the shard.

Our focus is on stability, fairness, and a long-lasting world that players can call home.
