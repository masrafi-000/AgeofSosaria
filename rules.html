Official Rules

Last updated: v1.0

Age of Sosaria is a long-term, custom Ultima Online shard focused on meaningful progression, fair play, and a healthy community.
Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans.

1

General Conduct

Players are expected to treat others with respect.

Harassment, hate speech, threats, or real-world targeting are not tolerated.

Disagreements and competition are part of the game; personal attacks are not.

Staff reserves the right to take action against behavior that harms the community.

2

Exploits & Abuse

Exploiting bugs or unintended behavior is prohibited.

Duping or system abuse will result in severe penalties.

Players are encouraged to report bugs immediately.

3

Automation

Unauthorized automation or scripts are prohibited.

Macros must comply with server limits.

Unfair AFK progression may be penalized.

4

Accounts

Max 3 accounts per player.

Account limit circumvention is not allowed.

All accounts are treated as one for enforcement.

5

Housing

One house per account.

Houses must be refreshed every 30 days.

Blocking public paths is forbidden.

6

Player Killing

PK is part of the game in Felucca.

No griefing (res-killing repeatedly without purpose).

7

AFK Macros

AFK resource gathering is allowed in houses only.

AFK combat training is prohibited.

8

Account Security

Players are responsible for their own accounts.

Losses from account sharing or negligence will not be restored.

9

Staff Authority

Staff decisions are final.

Punishments range from verbal warnings to permanent bans depending on severity.

10

Development

The shard is under active development.

Systems and balance may change.

Rollbacks or wipes may occur if absolutely necessary.
