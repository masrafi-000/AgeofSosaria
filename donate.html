Support Age of Sosaria

Age of Sosaria is a long-term custom Ultima Online shard built and maintained as a passion project. Donations are entirely optional and are used to help cover server costs, infrastructure, and ongoing development.

Supporting the shard helps ensure stability, longevity, and continued improvements for everyone.
